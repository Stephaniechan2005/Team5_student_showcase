Industry Night

Date:
Oct 2nd, 2024

Time:
EST 17:00 PM

Location:
Fanshawe College Downtown Campus

The SDPA Industry Night for 2023 will showcase student projects and activities from the Interactive Media Design Program. We extend a cordial invitation to an evening of professional engagement, featuring the presentation of Capstone Projects for Foundation Sixty6 and Student Portfolios. Attendees will have the opportunity to peruse and discuss the showcased work while enjoying a selection of catered food and refreshments.
